## Features

### Pages

1. **Home Page**
   - Hero section with call-to-action
   - Core services overview
   - AI Avatar placeholder
   - AI Business Score calculator
   - Why choose DharmaAI section
   - Mascot section

2. **Services Page**
   - AI Readiness Audit
   - Lead Generation Automation
   - AI Chatbots for Business
   - Digital AI Avatars
   - Workflow Automation
   - Pricing information

3. **Case Studies Page**
   - Local Business Automation case study
   - AI Lead Generation for Consultants case study
   - AI Chatbot for Customer Support case study
   - Metrics and results data

4. **AI Resource Hub**
   - Blog-style resource cards
   - Category filtering
   - Newsletter subscription
   - Educational content

5. **Contact Page**
   - Contact form with validation
   - Business information
   - Social media links
   - Booking placeholder
   - Map placeholder

### Technical Features

- Fully responsive design for all devices
- Sticky navigation bar
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Floating WhatsApp button (circular icon)
- AI chatbot simulation popup
- AI Business Score calculator
- Real notification system
- Form validation
- Smooth scrolling
- Resource filtering
- Newsletter subscription
